Pickering Point

Warrnambool, Victoria, Australia
  • Best time to shoot: Sunset.
  • Check suns location

Description: A great sunset location especially when the tide is on it's way out during summer as the sun sets further over the horizon, however this isn't just a summer location. Pickering Point can be shot year round just keep an eye on the tide. High tide can be a little unpredictable but will still turn out a great photo.

Pickering Point is easy to get to, just park in the McDonald st carpark, as you're heading towards Thunder Point turn left after the speed bump. Walk down the boardwalk to the left, towards Stingray Bay and climb down the stairs.

Hidden Gem: Down at Pickering Point head around the coast line a little to the right and you'll find a little cave. During Summer, a week either side of Christmas, the sun sets right in the middle of the opening making a great image.
